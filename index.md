## 🛠️ How it works

The xaf-raw-export-tool runs locally on your machine. When you open the application, it remains inside your browser environment. It reads your XAF file, translates the entries into a table, and allows you to save that table as a spreadsheet. Because the process happens on your computer, your financial data remains private and secure. You do not need to upload files to any external party.

## 💡 Frequently asked questions

**Does this tool save my data?**
No. The application does not store information. Once you close the browser tab or the program, the data from the XAF file is cleared from your system memory.

**Can I convert multiple files at once?**
Currently, the tool processes one file at a time. This keeps the performance stable and prevents errors during the conversion process.

**What is the difference between Excel and CSV?**
Excel files (.xlsx) preserve formatting and are ready for professional reports. CSV files are plain text files that offer compatibility with various accounting programs. Use the Excel option for general financial review.

**Do I need a constant internet connection?**
No. Once you have downloaded the tool, it operates entirely offline.
